Project notes for harvizzle.com

Aesthetic: minimal, white, serif, quiet, confident.
No clutter. No trends. Music first.

The site should feel like opening a book, not launching an app.

Priorities:
- Bandcamp embeds front and center on the Music page
- Navigation is simple and always visible
- No decorative elements unless they serve a clear purpose
- If in doubt, remove it

Future ideas (only if requested):
- Press/quotes section
- Tour dates
- Mailing list (simple, no pop-ups)
- Merch link
